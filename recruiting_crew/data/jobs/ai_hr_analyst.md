# AI HR Analyst

## Mission

We are hiring an AI HR Analyst to improve recruiting and people operations workflows using automation, data analysis, and AI-assisted tooling.

## Core responsibilities

- Analyze recruiting funnel data and identify bottlenecks.
- Design lightweight AI workflows for sourcing, screening, and internal HR operations.
- Partner with recruiters and HR business partners to improve process quality.
- Build reports for hiring leaders with clear recommendations.
- Document processes and train internal users on new workflows.

## Must-have requirements

- 3+ years in recruiting operations, people analytics, HR tech, or a similar role.
- Strong written communication in English and Spanish.
- Experience with ATS, spreadsheet analysis, and process documentation.
- Evidence of using automation, AI tools, SQL, Python, or no-code tools to improve workflows.

## Nice-to-have

- Experience evaluating prompts, copilots, or LLM-based workflows.
- Experience building dashboards for leadership.
- Exposure to change management or internal enablement.

## What success looks like in 90 days

- The hiring team receives a practical scorecard for candidate screening.
- Recruiting reporting becomes more consistent and faster.
- At least one manual HR workflow is redesigned with AI assistance.
